How to Play

The Big Bad Wolf is a survival card game where the last player standing wins.

🎴 Game Flow

  • Players take turns drawing cards.
  • If a Wolf card appears, it tries to eat you!
  • You must protect yourself using houses or meat.

🏠 Build Houses

  • 2 Hay → Hay House
  • 3 Wood → Wooden House
  • 3 Stone → Stone House

🐺 Wolves

  • Different wolves have different strengths.
  • Stronger houses protect you from stronger wolves.

🏆 Winning

Survive the wolves! The last player not eaten wins the game.
